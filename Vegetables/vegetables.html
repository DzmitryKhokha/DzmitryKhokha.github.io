Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 8 tomatoes, halved lengthwise
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Drizzle the olive oil over the cut sides of the tomatoes, and sprinkle with garlic, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Place tomatoes, cut sides up, onto the preheated grill, and grill until the tomatoes start to sizzle and show blackened grill marks, about 4 minutes. Flip the tomatoes over and grill until the garlic turns golden brown, about 3 more minutes.

Pepper

Ingredients:

  • 4 sweet peppers (red, yellow, orange, or purple bell peppers, or cubanelle peppers)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing
  • Salt, to taste, optional

Directions:

  1. Heat grill to medium-hot.
  2. While the grill heats, core and halve or quarter large peppers. Smaller peppers can be grilled whole.

Zucchini

Ingredients:

  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • red wine vinegar or balsamic
  • dried herbs such as parsley and basil
  • garlic powder, salt and pepper

Directions:

  1. To make perfect zucchini on a grill, start by seasoning your zucchini with oil, vinegar, herbs and spices.
  2. Heat the grill and make sure the grates are clean and well oiled.
  3. Grill until tender and grill marks appear.
